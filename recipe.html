Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate Chip cookies are delicious and can be enjoyed with a warm drink for the perfect combination

They also are very easy to make!

This video shows you the method to make these lovely cookies, and what they should look like at the end. The recipe is easily to follow but this video may help you visually see what each stage should look like.

Ingredients

  • 125g softened butter
  • 100g brown sugar
  • 125g caster sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 225g self raising flour
  • 200g chocolate chips

Method

  1. Cream butter and sugar
  2. Combine egg and vanilla
  3. Sift in Flour
  4. Add chocolate chips and mix
  5. Form cookie dough in to small balls and place on baking tray
  6. Bake at 180 degrees celcius for 10 minutes
  7. Allow to cool and serve

Nutritional Information per serving

  • 308 kcal
  • 16g fat
  • 10g saturates
  • 35g carbs
  • 21g sugars
  • 2g fibre
  • 3g protein
  • 0.2g salt
